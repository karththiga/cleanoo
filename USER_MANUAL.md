# Cleanoo User Manual

## 1) What is Cleanoo?
Cleanoo is a recycling pickup platform with two mobile user roles:
- **Households**: request waste pickups, track status, and earn recycling points.
- **Collectors**: accept assigned pickups, follow route workflow, and submit completion evidence.

---

## 2) Getting started

### Requirements
- Android phone with internet connection.
- Location services enabled (required when requesting pickup).
- A valid account:
  - Household users can self-register from the app.
  - Collector accounts are typically provisioned by the operations/admin team.

### First launch
1. Open the app.
2. On login, pick your role tab (**Household** or **Collector**).
3. Sign in with your email and password.

---

## 3) Household guide

### 3.1 Create an account (household only)
1. On login screen, keep **Household** tab selected.
2. Tap **Sign up**.
3. Enter name, email, phone, address, password, and confirm password.
4. Submit registration and return to login.

### 3.2 Log in
1. Select **Household** tab.
2. Enter email and password.
3. Tap **Login**.

### 3.3 Home screen
The Household Home screen shows:
- Greeting and your current point balance.
- Recent pickup requests (latest 3).
- Announcements.
- A **Request Pickup** action button.

### 3.4 Request a pickup
1. Tap **Request Pickup**.
2. Select waste category (Plastic, Paper, Glass, Metal, or E-Waste).
3. Allow location permission when prompted.
4. Drop a pin on the map (or move marker) to set pickup location.
5. Verify/edit address text.
6. Upload a waste image.
7. Optionally select preferred date/time.
8. Tap **Submit Request**.

> Important: Submission requires category selection, map pin, address, and image upload.

### 3.5 Track pickup status
1. Open **Pickups** tab.
2. Review current active job and full pickup history.
3. Tap any pickup card to open full details.

Typical statuses you may see:
- Pending pickup / waiting assignment.
- Collector on the way.
- Collector completed (awaiting your confirmation).
- Completed.

### 3.6 Confirm, cancel, complain, review
In **Pickups**:
- **Confirm Job Completed** appears when collector marks completion.
- **Cancel pickup** is available while request is still in early states.
- **Report issue** is available for delays or problems.
- For completed jobs, you can submit a **Review** and a **Complaint**.

### 3.7 Rewards and points
1. Open **Redeem Points** tab.
2. View available points and milestone hint.
3. Tap **View all rewards** to open rewards wallet summary.

### 3.8 Notifications
Use the **Notifications** tab to view system alerts and updates.

### 3.9 Profile and account settings
In **My Profile** you can:
- Update name, phone, address, and zone.
- Change password (minimum 6 characters).
- Log out.

---

## 4) Collector guide

### 4.1 Log in
1. On login screen, select **Collector** tab.
2. Enter collector email and password.
3. Tap **Login**.

### 4.2 Collector dashboard
Dashboard shows your active workflow:
- Current active pickup job.
- Pending assigned count.
- Main action button that changes with status.

### 4.3 Job workflow
Depending on status, actions are:
1. **Start route** (for assigned/approved jobs).
2. **Add evidence** (after pickup is marked in-progress/picked).
3. Job then proceeds for household confirmation and final completion.

You can also:
- Open **Job Details**.
- Submit **Collector Review**.
- Submit **Collector Complaint**.

### 4.4 History
Open **History** tab to view previous collector jobs.

### 4.5 Notifications
Open **Notifications** tab for updates.

### 4.6 Profile
In **My Profile**, collectors can:
- Update name, phone, and assigned zone.
- Change password.
- Log out.

---

## 5) Password reset
1. On login screen, tap **Forgot password**.
2. Enter your account email.
3. Tap **Reset**.
4. Check your inbox for the reset link.

---

## 6) Troubleshooting

### I cannot log in
- Confirm correct role tab is selected (Household vs Collector).
- Verify email/password.
- Reset password if needed.

### Pickup submission fails
- Ensure you selected waste category.
- Ensure a map pin is dropped.
- Ensure location/address is filled.
- Ensure an image is uploaded.
- Confirm internet connection and location services are enabled.

### Points are not updating
- Open Home or Redeem screen again to refresh.
- Sign out and sign back in if your session token expired.

### Profile updates fail
- Ensure required fields are not empty.
- Check internet connection.

---

## 7) Best practices
- Keep your address/phone current in profile.
- Upload clear waste photos for faster validation.
- Households should confirm completed jobs promptly.
- Collectors should keep route/evidence updates timely.
